Hobbies

My other big interest outside of music is world languages. I did 12 years of French and tried to teach myself German. I've picked up bits and pieces of other languages from music, most notably Italian. I know the Greek and Cyrillic alphabets and I'd like to learn to read Japanese, Hangul, Devanagari, and so on. Learning languages is fun, but what really gets me excited is just the notion of new languages in general. I love listening to different languages and learning about how languages changed and influenced each other over time. Just like music, language is a way to reflect emotions and experiences. There are things that can only be exactly expressed in certain languages, and I find that beautiful.

I had a big typology phase: my Myers-Briggs type is INTP, and my Enneagram is 9w1. I can still tell you what the cognitive functions for each of the 16 MBTI types are, or the core fears and motivations of the 9 Enneagram types. I remember relating to the INTP description a lot when I first discovered typology, and I got into a mindset of putting everyone into boxes for each personality type. It took a while for me to realize that typology was nothing more than pseudoscience and there wasn't any real merit to figuring out what type you were. People weren't black and white, people weren't just 4 letters and a number. Now and then, typology comes up in my conversations and I excitedly offer to assign them a personality. It's just for fun now, as it should be.

I also like Pokemon, Dungeons and Dragons, figure skating, Les Miserables, and writing!
